Company Profile
Who We Are

Harshal Engineering Services (HES) is a detail engineering firm founded in 1996, headquartered in Dombivali, Maharashtra. We specialize in full-cycle project execution services for small and medium-scale petrochemical, refinery, oil & gas, bulk drugs, pharma, heavy chemicals, dye & dye-intermediate, and power projects.

Over more than two decades, we have expanded to offer the complete range of detailed engineering, inspection and expediting, drawing conversion to CAD, construction supervision and management, manpower deputation, and recruitment for client & project management services across Piping, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and Instrumentation fields.

"More than 100 projects completed with reputed consulting & client companies across India."

Our infrastructure includes 20 AutoCAD workstations and 4 dedicated CAESAR II (v4.4) piping stress analysis software seats — enabling us to handle complex multi-discipline projects with speed and rigour.

Services Offered

Isometric Drawings
Isometrics for all lines above 2" dia.; smaller lines at discretion of engineer.

Pipe Stress Analysis

Material Take-Off (MTO)
