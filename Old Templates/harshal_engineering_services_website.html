Company Overview

Engineering Excellence
Since 1996

Harshal Engineering Services (HES) is a detailed engineering firm founded in 1996, specializing in Project Execution Services for Small & Medium size Petrochemical, Refinery, Oil & Gas, Bulk Drugs, Pharma, Heavy Chemicals, Dye & Dye-Intermediate Plants, and Power Projects.

Over the years, HES has expanded to offer a total range of Detailed Engineering, Inspection and Expediting, Drawing Conversion to CAD, Construction Supervision and Management, Manpower Deputation, and Recruitment for Client & Project Management Services.

"In more than a decade, our company has completed over 100 projects in the field of Detail Engineering with reputed Consulting & Client companies."

Our work spans Piping, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and Instrumentation fields — backed by 20 AutoCAD workstations and 4 CAESAR II (v4.4) piping stress analysis software licenses.
