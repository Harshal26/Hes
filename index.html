What We Do

Engineering Services & Capabilities

End-to-end detail engineering — from piping layouts to structural platforms and stress-certified designs.

Piping & Mechanical

Full-scope piping engineering and stress compliance for process plants

Piping System Engineering

Comprehensive layouts, equipment deck layouts, standard piping details, and isometric generation. Compatible with PDS & PDMS design workflows.

Piping Stress Analysis (CAESAR II)

Thermal, mechanical, and safety evaluation using CAESAR II. Pipe support and anchor design to prevent thermal expansion failures.

General Arrangement & Isometrics

GA drawings, MTO generation, 2D/3D layout coordination, and full bill of material packages for procurement.

P&ID & Process Integration

Translating P&ID process data into dimensioned plant layouts and safe piping configurations.

Civil, Structural & Manpower

Heavy infrastructure design and specialized technical staffing solutions

Civil & Structural Engineering

Technical Manpower Services

Strategic deployment of AutoCAD drafting specialists, CAD operators, designers, and project design engineers across India's refineries.

Equipment Foundation Design

Concrete foundation design for reactors, heat exchangers, columns, and rotating equipment per IS & ASME codes.

Commissioning Support

On-site technical staff during plant commissioning phases — stress resolution, punch list closure, and as-built documentation.
