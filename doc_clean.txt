	PIPING SYSTEM ENGINEERING
We take up piping system Engineering jobs for Chemical, fertilizers, pharmaceuticals, Petrochemicals and other type of industries. based on the basic Engineering details of the proposed projects. we work out various piping/mechanical/structural engineering test including following areas of design and selection.

BASIC ENGINEERING 
Design data 
p&i diagrams of process units showing the piping and instrumentation system for process and utilities with the following data.
Process equipment with elevation of critical equipments
Direction of flow.
Detail of process and utility piping including identification code numbers, piping specification, pipeline fitting ,sample points and process vents, traps and drains.
Line size, material of construction ,services and rating for all process and utility piping, valve and fitting.
Instruments list for instrumentation, hook-ups indication, measuring ,recording and control points.
Special sloping lines, critical elevation with respect to equipment or other pipe lines.
Jacketing ,tracing ,HDPE,PP,MSRL,MSGL etc.of each pipeline, pipe fitting, valve and equipment etc.
Hot ,pp and cold insulation.
Special agreements of piping from process consideration .

2.   BASIC INFORMATION ON EQUIPMENT / MATERIAL.
Heat exchangers giving material of construction and rating complete with thermal and process detail of tube sheets ,tube pitch, baffle spacing, nozzle sizes complete with thermal design put excluding complete mechanical design.
Tanks, vessels,reactors and columns giving dimensions ,list of connect icon detail of internal and material of constructions.
Piping valve, fitting ,level gauge ,sight glasses piping specifications indicating temperature rating, code requirements standard supports sketh,corrosion allowance flow rate and other characteristics.
Instrumentation indication basic specification, control loops, points of control, control variable, normal,minimum and maximum values of variables to be measured, recorded and controlled, material of construction,interlocks,pressure drops, across control valve, air filature under shutdown, emergency conditions and safety interlocks.
